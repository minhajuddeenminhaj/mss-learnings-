Top 3 current Cricketers

Virat Kohli is widely regarded as one of the greatest batsmen in the history of cricket. His records and achievements are unparalleled.

2. Babar Azam

Babar Azam is the captain of the Pakistan cricket team and is known for his elegant batting style and consistency across all formats.

3. ms dhoni
